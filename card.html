Interesting facts about pandas

  • Scientific name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average weight: 100 pounds (45 kgs)
  • Average lifespan: 20 years
  • Staple food: Bamboo and fruit

Pandas although indeginous to China, are on of the few indeginous wildlife species that were never hunted for use in traditional medicine. They also eat consistently and spend 12 - 14 hours a day eating.
